Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Click the "Enroll Now" button on any course page and complete the enrollment form. After submission, you'll receive confirmation and access instructions via email.

What are the prerequisites for courses?

Prerequisites vary by course. Beginner courses have no requirements, while advanced courses may require prior knowledge or completion of prerequisite courses. Check individual course descriptions for details.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for most courses. You can choose to pay in full or in monthly installments. Scholarships are also available for eligible students.

Are courses self-paced or scheduled?

We offer both formats. Self-paced courses allow you to learn on your own schedule, while cohort-based courses have specific start dates and deadlines to keep you on track with a group of peers.
